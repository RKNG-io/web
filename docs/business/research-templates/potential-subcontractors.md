## Evaluation Criteria

## Test Project Ideas

Small paid tasks to evaluate before committing to larger work:

1. **Simple Zap/n8n flow** - "When form submitted, add to Notion + send email" (£30-50)
2. **Xero invoice automation** - "Create invoice from Airtable row" (£50-100)
3. **Data sync** - "Sync Stripe customers to spreadsheet daily" (£40-80)
4. **Documentation** - "Document this existing workflow" (£30-50)

## Red Flags

- Takes >48hrs to respond to initial message
- Can't explain their work clearly
- No portfolio or examples
- Pushes back on small test project
- Rate seems too good to be true
- Copy-paste proposal that doesn't address your request
