# Launcher Questionnaire Flow

## Opening Screen

```
This is your moment.

You're here because something in you knows it's time.

Be honest with the next few questions. There are no wrong answers.
Just you, telling the truth about where you are and where you want to be.

Takes about 8 minutes. Worth it.

[Let's go →]
```

## Questions

### 1. Vision (Text)
**What does your life look like when this business is working?**
_Don't overthink it. Morning routine, how your days feel, what you've let go of, what you've built._

### 2. Who You Help (Text)
**Who do you help — and what changes for them?**
_Your clients, your people. What's different in their lives because of you?_

### 3. Business Type (Single Select)
**What kind of business are you building?**
- Coaching
- Creative Services
- Fitness / Wellness
- Food / Meal Prep
- Therapy / Counselling
- Professional Services
- Products / E-commerce
- Something else

### 4. Delivery Mode (Single Select)
**Will you work with clients online, in person, or both?**
- Mostly online / remote
- Mostly in person / local
- Both

### 5. Why Now (Multi Select)
**Why now?**
- I've been thinking about this for years and I'm done waiting
- Something changed — job, life, realisation
- I'm already doing this on the side and ready to go all in
- I'm not 100% sure it's "now" but I'm exploring

### 6. Business Stage (Single Select)
**How would you describe where you are today?**
- I have an idea but haven't started
- I've started but have no paying clients yet
- I have a few paying clients (1-5)
- I have regular clients but it's not my main income
- I'm already doing this full-time but want to level up

### 7. What's In Place (Multi Select)
**What do you have in place already?**
- Business name decided
- Business registered
- Business bank account
- Website or landing page
- Professional email
- Social media presence
- Email list
- Testimonials
- Insurance
- Contracts
- Accounting setup

### 8. Website Feeling (Single Select)
**When you imagine sending someone to your website, how do you feel?**
- Proud — it represents me well
- It's okay but needs work
- Embarrassed — it's outdated or doesn't exist
- I've been avoiding thinking about it

### 9. What's Missing (Multi Select)
**What feels most broken or missing right now?**
- I don't look professional enough online
- I don't know how to price my services
- I have no system for booking
- Taking payments feels awkward
- I'm not sure how to find clients
- I don't have contracts
- Everything is manual and exhausting
- I don't know what to prioritise

### 10. Blockers (Multi Select)
**What's actually stopping you from being further along?**
- Time — I'm still working another job
- Money — I can't invest much yet
- Knowledge — I don't know the steps
- Confidence — I'm not sure I'm ready
- Tech — I find the tools overwhelming
- Perfectionism — I keep waiting until it's "right"
- Fear — what if it doesn't work?
- Nothing specific — I just haven't done it

### 11. Core Fear (Single Select)
**What's the fear underneath the fear?**
- What if I'm not good enough?
- What if no one wants what I'm offering?
- What if I fail publicly?
- What if I succeed and can't handle it?
- What if I invest and it doesn't pay off?
- I'm not sure — just general anxiety
- Honestly? I'm not that scared. I'm ready.

### 12. Timeline (Single Select)
**When do you want to be "live"?**
- Already am, just need to improve
- Within the next month
- Within 3 months
- Within 6 months
- No fixed timeline — exploring

### 13. Time Available (Single Select)
**How much time can you realistically dedicate each week?**
- Less than 2 hours
- 2-5 hours
- 5-10 hours
- 10+ hours
- I'm going full-time

### 14. Budget (Single Select)
**What's your comfort zone for investing?**
- Under £100
- £100-300
- £300-500
- £500-1,000
- £1,000+
- I'm not sure yet

### 15. One Year Vision (Text)
**Imagine it's one year from today. What needs to be true for you to feel proud?**

### 16. One Thing (Text)
**What's the one thing you most want help with?**

### 17. Anything Else (Text, Optional)
**Anything else you want me to know?**

```

[Show me my Reckoning →]
```
